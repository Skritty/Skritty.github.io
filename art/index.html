Environments, Models, and Art

Ritual Site

KeySupport 1Support 2VideoInformation

Lightly inspired by the Thaumcraft mod for Minecraft made by Azanor. A large magical silverwood tree sits at the center of a ring of standing stones, where wisps from other trees gather it’s vis. It is set in a foggy magical forest by a lake. The wisps fight over vis and act in a natural way. The wisp simulation can be found on the simulations page: https://trevorthacker.com/tools/

Cathedral

KeySupport 1Support 2VideoInformation

Created in my Virtual Environments class. We were given random shapes and had to build an interesting interior environment. I decided that the arches and pillars would make a great cathedral. There are no textures, but I added lighting, particles, and effects like flickering candle light and lightning from the outside that lights up the entire room for a very short amount of time.
